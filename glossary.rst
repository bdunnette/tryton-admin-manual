Glossary
========

.. glossary::

   party
      Any entity (individual or corporate) that one might transact business with.

   wizard
      A sequence of dialog boxes that leads the :term:`user` through a series of well-defined steps.
	
   rule
      A set of criteria that controls access to a :term:`model` or :term:`field`.

   model
      A representation of a particular set of data and processes, such as a :term:`party` or :term:`sale`.

   company
      In Tryton, a *company* is an entity that is "internal" to the system (in other words, a company whose data is being stored in Tryton); any "external" company would be represented as a :term:`party`.
